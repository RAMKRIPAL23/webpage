Web design and development is the process of creating a website. It involves two
major skill sets: web design and web development. Web design determines the
look and feel of a website, while web development determines how it functions
Web developers Make and maintain websites. They are in charge of a site's
overall look and feel.They also handle the technical aspects of a website, including
its performance and capacity.
